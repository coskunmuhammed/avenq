# AVENQ Launch Assets & Production Package v2.0

Master specification and design templates for institutional assets including pitch decks, one-pagers, business cards, email signatures, press kits, and Open Graph meta graphics.

---

## 1. Production Package Inventory

### Institutional Communications
- **Company One-Pager**: Minimalist single-sheet PDF detailing AVENQ ecosystem, capabilities, and contact points.
- **Pitch Deck Template**: 10-slide monochrome deck (16:9 widescreen, dark mode default, `#0B0B0B` background, high-contrast typography).
- **Business Cards**: 85mm x 55mm matte black cardstock, debossed white wordmark and monospaced contact telemetry.
- **Email Signature**:
  ```text
  NAME // ROLE
  AVENQ — Technology Company
  contact@avenq.pro | https://avenq.pro
  ```

### Digital Brand Assets
- **SVG Symbol Package**: Located in `public/assets/brand/`:
  - `symbol-monochrome.svg`
  - `wordmark-horizontal.svg`
  - `monogram-app-icon.svg`
- **Favicon Package**: Multi-resolution favicons (`16x16`, `32x32`, `180x180` Apple Touch Icon, `512x512` web app manifest icon).
- **Open Graph Image**: `1200x630` PNG with high-contrast logo and monospaced tagline (*"Software built to endure."*).
