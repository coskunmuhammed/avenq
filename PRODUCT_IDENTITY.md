# AVENQ Product Identity System — Standards & Architecture v2.0

The Product Identity System governs how individual platforms (e.g., SATKIRALA, QR MENU) inherit AVENQ's global brand authority while maintaining distinct operational identities.

---

## 1. Product Naming Conventions
- **All-Caps Moniker**: Public product names are rendered in uppercase, spaced monospaced font or high-weight sans-serif (e.g., `SATKIRALA`, `QR MENU`).
- **Core Definition**: Every product must have a single-sentence definition (*"The digital operating infrastructure for real estate transactions"*).
- **Core Stance**: Every product carries a single philosophical principle (*"Trust requires structure, not advertising"*).

## 3. Shared Product OS Components
Every product in the ecosystem shares:
- Global Navigation bar & Footer schema
- Unified authentication & identity token management
- Monospaced telemetry status indicators (`status: operational`)
- Accessible form controls (`Input`, `Textarea`, `Button`)
- High-performance static page generation & API routes
