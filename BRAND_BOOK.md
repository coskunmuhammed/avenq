# AVENQ Brand Book — Official Brand & Visual System Guidelines v2.0

This Brand Book defines the global visual identity, verbal tone, asset usage, and communication standards for AVENQ.

---

## 1. Brand Philosophy
AVENQ is a technology company. We build software products, business platforms, AI-powered systems, and cloud infrastructure.
We build software companies, not client projects. We build products instead of selling hours.

---

## 2. Voice & Tone Rules
- **Direct & Uncompromising**: Avoid soft claims, agency filler, or marketing fluff.
- **Forbidden Vocabulary**:
  - ❌ "Revolutionary", "Cutting-edge", "Game-changing", "Seamless", "Next-gen"
  - ❌ "Digital transformation experts", "End-to-end solutions", "Custom software for everyone"
- **Approved Tone**:
  - ✅ *"Software built to endure."*
  - ✅ *"Our capabilities extend beyond our public products."*
  - ✅ *"Technology should solve operational problems—not create new ones."*

---

## 3. Visual Language
- **Monochrome & High Contrast**: Black background (`#0B0B0B`), charcoal surface (`#141414`), pure white text (`#FFFFFF`), light gray subtext (`#888888`).
- **Architectural Lines**: Fine 1px subtle borders (`rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`).
- **Photography Style**: Factual, high-contrast, structural photography. No stock photos of people smiling at laptops.
- **Illustration Rules**: Zero decorative vector art, zero isometric 3D shapes, zero corporate vector characters. Use structural diagrams, telemetry graphs, and monospaced code snippets.

---

## 4. Logo Usage & Clear Space
- **Symbol**: Geometric 16px crisp square monogram.
- **Clear Space**: Minimum padding around the logo equal to 50% of the symbol's height.
- **Minimum Size**:
  - Symbol: `16px x 16px`
  - Horizontal Wordmark: `80px` width
- **Incorrect Usage**:
  - ❌ Do not apply drop shadows, glows, or gradients.
  - ❌ Do not stretch or distort the ratio.
  - ❌ Do not place on colorful or busy photographic backgrounds.

---

## 5. Color Palette

```
#0B0B0B  Base Background
#141414  Surface Container
#1C1C1C  Elevated Surface
#FFFFFF  Primary Text & High Contrast Elements
#888888  Secondary Description Text
#555555  Captions & Telemetry Metadata
#10B981  Status Active / Operational
#FBBF24  Status Warning / In Development
#F87171  Status System Exception
```

---

## 6. Social Media & Presentation Rules
- **Post Layout**: Monospaced category tag at top (`// ENGINEERING NOTE`), heavy headline, short factual body.
- **Carousels**: Dark charcoal cards with white borders. Single idea per slide.
- **Business Cards**: 350gsm matte black cardstock, debossed white typography, monospaced details.
- **Email Signature**:
  ```
  NAME // TITLE
  AVENQ — Technology Company
  contact@avenq.pro | https://avenq.pro
  ```
